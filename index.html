App Guide

Song List Icons:

  • 🎙️ (Bright): In your current active setlist.
  • 🎙️ (Faded): In a different saved setlist.
  • 🔥: Trending (3+ plays in last 6 months).
  • 🗓️ >2y: Legacy song (Pre-December 2023 history).
  • ⛶ Tap on any song card to expand it and view details.

Pro Tips:

  • Long Press: Tap and hold on a song card to add it to favorites or a setlist.
  • Tap the BPM: Filter songs to similar tempos.
  • Swipe from left: Open filters drawer.
  • Swipe from right: See options and sort.
  • Swipe up from search bar: Shortcut to open the Setlist Builder.
  • Thinking outside the box: Quickly close a window by tapping outside of it. Try it out!
